Hi. I'm Ben.

I'm a fiction writer interested in the American west and Japan across literary and dystopian narratives. My work has most recently been featured in Lime Hawk and Cirque: A Literary Journal for Alaska and the Pacific Northwest.

I'm also a Staff Writer for PlayerTheory.com, where I focus on features about the gaming industry and the people who build it.

In a past life, I was a strategist and copy writer for members of Congress, local mayors and officials, non-profits, and candidates for public office across America.

I live in Portland, Oregon (by way of Los Angeles) with my wife and daughter. I like bicycles, sumo wrestling, tiki bars, coffee shops, whiskey, nature, cats, tabletop games, video games with good stories, and the Los Angeles Dodgers.
